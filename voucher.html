Bein Connect
Voucher Redeem

New Customers:

  • Visit https://connect.bein.com/subscribe or click on Subscribe in the top navigation bar.
  • Enter your voucher in the dedicated section “I have a Promo Code” and click on “Redeem”.
  • Click on “Next”.
  • Fill out all required information and click on “Next” to submit your details.
  • From your inbox, click on the link found in the email that you received in  order to verify your account.
  • After you click on the activation button in the email, your account will be activated and you will be redirected to the confirmation page, click on start 
  • watching to view live streaming.

Existing customers:

  • Visit https://connect.bein.com/sign-in.
  • Sign in using your existing beIN CONNECT account username and password then click on “Subscribe” in the Home Page.
  • Enter your voucher in “I have a Promo Code” box and click on Redeem.
  • Re-check the information and Click on “Next”.
  • After you click on “Next”, your account will be activated and you will be  redirected to the confirmation page.
  • Click on start watching to view live streaming.
